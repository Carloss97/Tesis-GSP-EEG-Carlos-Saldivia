\section{Conclusion}

This paper draft reports the current B2 stage of our EEG reconstruction benchmark. The main result is that graph-based and TV/time methods remain strong candidates under scenario-based missing-channel protocols, with consistent performance across MAE, RMSE, DTW, and SNR.

Our current position is cautious: we already have consolidated full-scale artifacts for publication drafting, but we still need to close strict MATLAB/GSPBox equivalence for BGSRP and run final submission-level ablations.

In short, B2 gives enough evidence to move forward with manuscript writing, while keeping transparent what is still pending before final submission.
