\section{Results}
The final comparative pack (tag: \showresfile{results/it100_final_comparative_publication_pack_run_metadata.json}) reported a median gain of \ItHundredGainPct\% (field \texttt{qa.gain\_pct}) and a highly significant two-sided test ($p=\ItHundredPval$); the metadata lists \texttt{\ItHundredBestMethod} as the best-performing method with best\_mae = \ItHundredBestMae. All per-iteration metrics and the consolidation scripts are stored in the \texttt{results/} directory for reproducibility and independent verification.

\subsection{Iterative coverage (it02--it130)}
The project's iteration campaign covers it02--it130 with a comprehensive inventory of per-iteration artifacts. The consolidated report lists a total of 90 GO iterations across the campaign (see the project inventory: \showresfile{Thesis-Copilot-Toolkit/ITERATIONS_COMPREHENSIVE_REPORT.md}). Phases it02--it70 produced the canonical synthetic and proxy validations with consistent GO results favoring TV/time methods; it71--it100 expanded coverage to few-missing scenarios and cross-dataset generalization (engine v7/v7e). The multidomain expansion (it105--it120) produced mixed outcomes and several NO-GO decisions at the aggregated gate — these per-iteration decisions and p-values are recorded in the results CSVs (examples: \showresfile{results/b3_stat02_significance.csv}, \showresfile{results/b3_rep01_final_table_overall.csv}).

For transparency, Table~\ref{tab:rep01} and the concise claim-traceability table provide direct links from high-level claims to the corresponding artifacts. The repository also contains full long-form traceability and a CSV export for machine validation (\showresfile{paper/ieee/tables/claim_traceability_export.csv}).
