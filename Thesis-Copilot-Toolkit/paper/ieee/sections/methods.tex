\section{Methods}

We represent EEG channels as graph vertices and model inter-channel relationships with weighted adjacency matrices. Missing channels are reconstructed with multiple interpolation families.

\subsection{Graph Construction}
We evaluate neighborhood and fully connected schemes, including kNN-style and NNK-inspired constructions.

\subsection{Interpolation Families}
We compare classical baselines, graph smoothness priors, and temporal regularization methods.

\subsection{Evaluation Metrics}
Performance is assessed with MAE, RMSE, DTW, and SNR.
