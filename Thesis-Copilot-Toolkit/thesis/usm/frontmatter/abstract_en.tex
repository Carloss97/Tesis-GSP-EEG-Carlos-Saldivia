\chapter*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}

This thesis studies EEG channel reconstruction under missing-channel scenarios using graph signal processing methods, classical interpolation baselines, and temporal regularization strategies. A reproducible evaluation pipeline is built to compare method families under controlled and realistic masking protocols. Results are reported with MAE, RMSE, DTW, and SNR to identify when graph-based methods offer robust practical improvements.
