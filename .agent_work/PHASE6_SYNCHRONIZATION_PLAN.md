## Overview
Phase 6 aligns the paper and thesis so that both documents tell the same story, use the same evidence, and preserve numeric and narrative consistency after Phases 2-5.

---

## Phase 6 Objectives

### 6.1: Structural Mapping
**Goal**: Build a paper-to-thesis section mapping matrix.

**Steps**:
1. Identify corresponding sections in paper and thesis.
2. Map each paper subsection to the matching thesis chapter or subsection.
3. Record missing or thesis-only/paper-only material.
4. Flag any duplicated claims that should be condensed.

**Success Criteria**:
- A complete mapping matrix exists for all major sections.
- Section coverage gaps are explicitly listed.

### 6.2: Numeric Consistency Review
**Goal**: Verify that shared numbers and claims are identical across both documents.

**Steps**:
1. Cross-check dataset sizes, sample counts, method counts, and comparison counts.
2. Compare all reported metrics and summary claims.
3. Confirm that tables, captions, and prose use the same values.
4. Flag any divergence for correction.

**Success Criteria**:
- No numeric contradictions between paper and thesis.
- Any intentional differences are documented.

### 6.3: Narrative Alignment
**Goal**: Ensure the conclusion, limitations, and contribution statements are aligned.

**Steps**:
1. Compare introduction, results, and conclusion narratives.
2. Verify that scope statements match the evidence in both documents.
3. Ensure tone and emphasis are consistent.
4. Harmonize wording where one document is stronger or weaker than the other without changing meaning.

**Success Criteria**:
- Paper and thesis express the same conclusions with matching scope.
- Any differences are intentional and documented.

### 6.4: Final Synchronization Validation
**Goal**: Recompile both documents after synchronization edits and verify no regressions.

**Steps**:
1. Recompile paper and thesis.
2. Confirm that bibliography remains clean.
3. Confirm that cross-references and citations still resolve.
4. Record final sizes and any outstanding gaps.

**Success Criteria**:
- Both documents compile cleanly.
- Phase 6 changes do not reintroduce Phase 5 issues.
